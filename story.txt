There is a universe far, far away call the Ruineterra. And there's a huge kingdom call Demacia. Their country was build upon an ancient war cause by magic. The 
survivors of the war fled to this piece of land and build Demacia. Hence, the people and their children sees magic as a sort of "evil" power, and it is prohibited
in Demacia. In this kingdom, there are two families who guards the kingdom for centuries. One of them are the royal family of the kingdom, and the other one 
protects the royal family. But one day, a baby girl who was borned in this family, finds out she has the power of magic herself...
